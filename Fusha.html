Fusha
Summary:

Fusha is another great asain resturant in mainstreet, Freehold. I definetley reccomend this resturant if you are looking for more diverse options when eating. Fusha has everything you can think of when your looking for Asain cusine. Fusha has everything from noodles, sushi, rice, and a lot more. The resturant has great food and customer service what stands out about this resturant is how crampt it is, you may either love this or hate this aspect of this resturant but in my opinion I feel like the cramptness in the resturant truly expresses the Japanese culture in this resturant
