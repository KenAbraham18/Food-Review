P.F. Chang's
Summary:

P.F. Changs is another great Asian resturant, located at the Freehold Raceway Mall. The food there is great and the customer service is also great but the thing that pops out the most in the resturant is most definetley the resturant aesthetic. outside of the restuareant you are greeted with horse statues that were made beautifully and when you step inside the resturant, you enter an extremley cozy enviornment that puts you in a relaxed state, the food there is also a relatively cheap price.
